## Getting Started

### How do I activate my AI receptionist?

1. **You'll receive a welcome email** with your new Trade Receptionist number (e.g., `+44 7728 407580`)
2. **Dial the divert code** on your mobile to forward unanswered calls:
   - Open your phone's keypad
   - Type exactly: `**004*+447728407580#`
   - Press call
   - You should hear a confirmation tone
3. **Test it** — let a call ring to voicemail, then the receptionist will answer it on the second ring
4. **Complete your profile** in the dashboard (business name, trade type, hours) so the agent sounds personal

### What's the divert code again?

**`**004*+447728407580#`** (use your actual number from the welcome email)

- Works on EE, O2, Vodafone, Three
- Takes 30 seconds
- Can be disabled anytime with `##004#`

**Still not working?**
- Make sure you dialled it *exactly* — no spaces, no typos
- Try on a different network (some MVNOs don't support it)
- Restart your phone and try again
- [Contact support](#support)

## Call Handling

### What happens when someone calls my number?

1. **Receptionist answers in 1–2 rings** (always answers, 24/7)
2. **Asks what they need** — job, quote, emergency, etc.
3. **If it's a job:**
   - Asks their name, postcode, phone, job type
   - **Checks your diary** for available slots
   - **Books a time** if you want them to
   - Sends you an SMS + email summary
4. **If it's not urgent:** Takes a message, you get a WhatsApp summary

### Can I review the call afterward?

**Yes.** Log into your dashboard:
- **Calls** tab → see transcript, duration, caller details, outcome
- **Leads** tab → see all bookings & enquiries with contact info
- **Fully automated lead capture** — no data entry needed

### The agent didn't answer / gave the wrong info

- **No answer:** Rare, but if it happens, you'll get a voicemail. Email us immediately so we can investigate.
- **Wrong info given:** The agent learns from feedback. Reply to the WhatsApp summary or email us the call ID.

### Can I hear the call recording?

**Yes.** Go to **Calls** tab in the dashboard, click the call, and there's a link to the recording.

## Calendar & Booking

### The agent says "I can't see the diary" — why?

**Most common cause:** Calendar not connected to your account.

1. Go to **Settings** → **Connect Google Calendar**
2. Sign in with Google
3. Choose the calendar you use for jobs (usually "Primary")
4. Grant permission
5. Set your business hours (e.g., 08:00–17:00 Mon–Fri)

**If you already did this:**
- Your calendar app may have revoked permission. Reconnect in Settings.
- Check that your Google account has access to the calendar (shared calendars sometimes don't sync).
- Check your time zone is correct in Settings.

### Bookings aren't appearing in my calendar

- **Connection issue:** Reconnect in Settings (step above)
- **Permissions issue:** Make sure your Google Calendar app allows Trade Receptionist to write events
- **Time zone mismatch:** If you see bookings at the wrong time, check Settings → Time Zone

**If it's still broken:** Email us the call ID and we'll check the logs.

### Can the agent book into multiple calendars?

**Not yet.** It syncs to your primary calendar. If you use multiple calendars, we recommend syncing them all to one view in Google Calendar settings.

### What if I'm fully booked?

The agent will say so and offer a waiting list. You control the availability — set your business hours in Settings, and the agent only offers real free slots.

## Technical Issues

### I'm not getting WhatsApp summaries

1. Make sure you provided a **UK mobile number** when the agent asked
2. Check your WhatsApp is set to receive messages from unknown numbers (doesn't always block Trade Receptionist)
3. Go to **Settings** → check your phone number is stored correctly

**No fix?** Email us and we'll resend manually.

### I'm not getting email summaries

1. Check your **spam/junk folder** (corporate firewalls sometimes block them)
2. In Settings, confirm your email address is correct
3. Try adding `no-reply@tradereceptionist.com` to your contacts

**Still missing?** Email us with the call ID.

### My calls aren't showing up in the dashboard

- Wait 2–3 minutes after the call ends (async processing)
- Refresh the page
- Log out and back in
- Check you're looking at the right business account (if you have multiple)

**Still missing?** Email us the caller's number and the time they called.

## The AI Receptionist

### What can it do?

- Answer calls 24/7
- Take caller details (name, postcode, phone, job type)
- Check your calendar and offer real available slots
- Book jobs into your calendar
- Send SMS & email summaries
- Handle basic enquiries (quotes, callbacks, emergencies)
- Learn from feedback (improves over time)

### What can't it do?

- Take payment (tells caller to pay on-site or in person)
- Handle complex multi-step jobs (refers to you)
- Access voicemail or call recordings from your phone
- Make outbound calls
- Integrate with your CRM (data comes via dashboard & WhatsApp)

### Can I customize what it says?

**Limited right now.** You can set:
- Your business name
- Your trade (plumbing, electrical, etc.)
- Your business hours
- Whether you want bookings confirmed SMS/email

Full customization (tone, greeting, questions) coming soon.

### Does it handle emergencies?

Yes. If the caller says "emergency," it flags the lead as urgent and you get an SMS immediately. You decide if it's real.

## Data & Privacy

### Where is my data stored?

**UK servers** (Supabase, hosted in EU region near UK). GDPR compliant.

### Can I export my leads?

**Yes.** Go to **Leads** → click the download button (top right). CSV format, ready for Excel or your CRM.

### What about call recordings?

- Stored securely for 30 days
- You can delete anytime in the dashboard
- Never shared with third parties

### Do you listen to my calls?

No. The AI handles the call; we don't monitor it. Recordings are encrypted at rest.
