# Trade Receptionist — Quick Start (5 minutes)

You've got a 14-day free trial of your AI receptionist. Here's how to get it live.

---

## Step 1: Get Your Number (in email)

Your welcome email has:
- Your unique Trade Receptionist number (e.g., `+44 7728 407580`)
- A divert code (e.g., `**004*+447728407580#`)
- A link to your dashboard

**Save these.** You'll need them in 30 seconds.

---

## Step 2: Activate Call Diverting (2 minutes)

This forwards your unanswered calls to your AI receptionist.

**On any UK mobile:**

1. Open the phone **Keypad**
2. Type this exactly (no spaces): `**004*YOUR_NUMBER#`
   - Replace `YOUR_NUMBER` with the number from your email
   - Example: `**004*+447728407580#`
3. Press **Call**
4. You'll hear a tone confirming it worked

**Done.** From now on, calls you don't answer ring the receptionist instead.

**Note:** This works on EE, O2, Vodafone, and Three. If your network doesn't support it, email us.

---

## Step 3: Set Up Your Profile (2 minutes)

Log into your dashboard:

1. Click **Settings**
2. Fill in:
   - **Business name** (e.g., "Smith Plumbing")
   - **Your name** (e.g., "Mark")
   - **Trade** (plumbing, electrical, etc.)
   - **Business hours** (e.g., 08:00–17:00 Mon–Fri)
   - **Phone number** (for SMS summaries)
3. Click **Save**

That's it. The agent now knows who you are.

---

## Step 4: Connect Your Calendar (1 minute)

So the receptionist can check your availability and book jobs.

1. Go to **Settings** → **Connect Google Calendar**
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Choose your calendar (usually "Primary")
4. Grant permission
5. You're done — the agent can now see your free slots

---

## Step 5: Test It (optional, but recommended)

### Option A: Free browser test (no phone needed)

1. Go to **Settings** → **Test Agent**
2. Click **▶ Start test call**
3. Allow microphone access
4. Talk to your receptionist live in your browser
5. Say: *"I need a tap repair on Tuesday afternoon"*
6. Watch it check your calendar and book a slot

### Option B: Real phone test

1. **Wait 5 minutes** after activating the divert (takes time to propagate)
2. Call your Trade Receptionist number from another phone
3. Say: *"Hi, I need help with a boiler repair"*
4. Receptionist asks for your name, postcode, phone, and books a time if available

---

## Step 6: See Your First Lead

After the test call (real or browser):

1. Log into the dashboard
2. Go to **Calls** tab → see the transcript and call recording
3. Go to **Leads** tab → see the booking with caller details

No data entry needed — everything's captured automatically.

## Common First Questions

### The divert code didn't work

- Dial it again — make sure it's exact: `**004*YOUR_NUMBER#`
- Try from a different network (some MVNOs don't support it)
- Restart your phone and try again
- If still stuck, [email support](mailto:hello@tradereceptionist.com)

### The agent can't see my calendar

In Settings, make sure:
- You've connected Google Calendar
- You granted permission
- Your time zone is correct

Then reconnect if needed.

### I haven't received an SMS after a call

- Make sure you gave your UK mobile number to the agent
- Check your spam folder
- Make sure your WhatsApp allows messages from unknown numbers
- Wait 2 minutes (it takes time to send)

### I want to test without calling my real number

Go to **Settings** → **Test Agent** → click the button. It's free, uses your browser mic, and runs against your live setup.
