1. Open your phone's **Keypad** (not the phone icon — the dial pad)

**If it still doesn't work:**

- Restart your phone and try once more

**If none of that works,** reply with:
- Your mobile network (EE/O2/Vodafone/Three/other)
- A screenshot of the error (if any)

We'll sort it from there.

## Calendar Not Connecting

**Subject:** Google Calendar sync — quick fix

Hi [Name],

The receptionist needs access to your calendar to book jobs. Here's how to reconnect:

**In your dashboard:**

1. Go to **Settings** → **Connect Google Calendar**
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Select the calendar you use for jobs (usually "Primary")
4. Click **Allow** when Google asks for permission
5. Check your **Business hours** are correct

**After reconnecting:**
- Wait 2 minutes for sync
- Try another test call
- The agent should now see your availability

**If it's still stuck:**
- Make sure your Google account actually has access to that calendar (shared calendars sometimes need extra setup)
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting
- Reply with your time zone — it might be set wrong

Let me know if this helps.

Best,  
Trade Receptionist Support

Sometimes it takes a moment for calls to appear. Try this:

1. Wait **2–3 minutes** (we process in the background)
2. **Refresh** your browser (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R)
3. **Log out and back in** (clears cache)
4. Check you're in the right **business account** (if you have multiple)

**If it's still missing:**
- Go to the Retell dashboard and confirm the call was answered (not a failed connection)
- Reply with:
  - The caller's number (or "anonymous")
  - The exact time they called
  - Your phone number

We can trace it from there and add it manually if needed.

## Escalation Path

If you can't solve it:

1. **Gather:** Call ID, time, what happened, screenshots
2. **Check logs:** (if you have Railway/Sentry access) Look for errors
3. **Document:** Add to a shared "Support Issues" list for review
4. **Reply to customer:** "We're looking into this — should have an answer by [TIME]"

Never say "we don't know" — say "we're investigating and will have an answer by X."
